Design Leadership Is Decision Quality

Why senior design impact is measured by decision quality, not artifact volume.

1 min read

Design leadership is less about final pixels and more about improving how teams decide.

Good leaders make trade-offs explicit, document rationale, and build systems that reduce future ambiguity.

Teams move faster when decisions are visible, revisitable, and tied to shared goals.
