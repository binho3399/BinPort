Portfolio CTA Optimizer

Built an experiment to test CTA placement and wording across portfolio pages.

1 min readOpen demoView repository

## Hypothesis

CTAs placed immediately after project outcomes can increase contact intent.

## Build

Created two CTA variants and tracked click-through by page depth.

## What worked

Variant B with proof-first copy showed stronger click rates.

## What failed

Sticky CTA bar added noise and hurt reading depth.

## Next iteration

Test personalized CTA labels by visitor segment.
