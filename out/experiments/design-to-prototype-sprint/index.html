Design-to-Prototype Sprint

A 48-hour sprint to convert a product concept into a clickable coded prototype.

1 min readOpen demoView repository

## Hypothesis

Constrained prototype timelines force better prioritization.

## Build

Translated core flows into coded prototype with realistic transitions.

## What worked

## What failed
