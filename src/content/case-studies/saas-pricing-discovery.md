## Context
B2B SaaS product had expanding usage but weak conversion from trial to paid.

## Problem
Prospects could not clearly map each plan to business value and expected outcomes.

## Strategic artifacts
- Problem framing linked pricing confusion to sales cycle delays.
- Segment-specific JTBD analysis identified value mismatch in mid-tier positioning.
- Roadmap proposal prioritized pricing page first, then in-app upgrade moments.

## Design artifacts
- Reframed plans around outcomes instead of feature count.
- Built comparison model highlighting "best fit" path per company size.
- Standardized pricing UI patterns for consistency across web and app.

## Proof artifacts
- Before and after heatmaps showed improved attention to value sections.
- Weekly growth report confirmed sustained conversion lift after launch.
- Sales team feedback cited cleaner conversations and fewer clarification calls.

## Trade-offs
We removed a complex calculator tool that looked advanced but created decision fatigue.

## Learnings
When value communication improves, product growth and sales efficiency move together.
