Vibe coding is not about replacing engineering depth. It is about reducing ambiguity before handoff.

The strongest workflow is: define hypothesis, ship a focused prototype, observe behavior, then decide.

Designers who can prototype outcomes earn influence earlier in roadmap conversations.
