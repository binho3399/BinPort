# Launch and Optimization Plan

## Launch checklist
- Confirm all featured case studies and experiments are published.
- Validate metadata for every page and article.
- Verify sitemap and robots endpoints.
- Verify CTA links and event tracking names.
- Run performance and accessibility checks.

## Analytics events
- `click_contact_cta`
- `download_cv`
- `click_contact_email`
- `click_contact_linkedin`
- `open_project_demo` (for external demo links)
- `case_study_read_50`
- `case_study_read_75`

## Weekly dashboard
- Top landing pages
- Traffic sources
- CTA conversion rates
- Case study engagement depth

## Optimization cadence
- Every 2 weeks, adjust hero copy, project ordering, and CTA wording.
- Every month, retire low-impact entries and feature stronger proof artifacts.
- Every quarter, refresh About narrative and positioning signals.
