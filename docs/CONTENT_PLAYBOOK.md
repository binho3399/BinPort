## Publishing workflow
1. Draft content in relevant collection.
2. Run quality checklist.
3. Publish with complete metadata.
4. Share to LinkedIn or other channel.
5. Revisit after new outcomes and update impact.

## Quality checklist
- Narrative is clear and concise.
- Includes proof, metrics, or outcome signal.
- Includes a clear CTA path (contact, email, or CV).
- Includes tags and category metadata.
- Includes date and summary for discoverability.
